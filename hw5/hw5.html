You can notice that the range of the distribution get smaller when the standard deviation changes from 20 to 5, and the bins need to be smaller for the distribution to look like a bell curve, or a higher resolution. The opposite happens when it moves from 20 to 50 -- the range becomes much wider, and bigger bins help make curve look like a bell.
